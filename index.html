About

In the mid-nineties I worked as a production assistant on independent films, commercials and in the early stages of reality televison. I left the business and pursued other employment prospects. Upon reaching reaching my late 40's, I decided to fulfill a goal I had abandoned in my early years, I went back to college. I also realized how much I missed the experience of working on film or video projects and then seeing the result on a screen. I was always fascinated by pictures as far as I can remember. Taking an image and adding more images to tell a story in greater depth fascinates me. Media allows us to scream out to to the world our ideas, and opinions about life. It also allows us to create imaginary worlds. I enjoy listening to the ideas of others and working together to bring those ideas to life. Editing a project allows you to change the perspective of a character and tell the story in any manner desired. To put together the scenes and helping the director or writer’s vision come to life is a challenge I welcome.

As time progressed in college, I was able to appreciate another form of storytelling. I guess my fondness for film and photography led me to another form of media, animation. I enjoyed it as a child, but upon learning hands-on how it was done, it opened my mind to the idea of telling a story in such a manner. Animation allows you to create a different world. The perspective and portrayal of characters is different when done in a real-world production.

New Media to me means unlimited creativity. I look forward to others sharing their creativity with me and I will do the same. Sometimes in life one has to be a leader. Sometimes one has to be a follower. Whatever the New Media project requires, I will become the individual that is needed for that project’s success.
