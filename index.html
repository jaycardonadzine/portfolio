About

Life experience is the most powerful influence in one’s art. Whether it is photography, film, or animation; our exposure to past events shapes us into what our minds create and how we see things. Growing up as a child of the 1970’s and a teenager of the 1980’s, I witnessed many changes. I lived in a world that lacked internet, websites, music downloads, smartphones, streaming, and technological social networks. It is not just family, or our social circle that affects us, but the technology that exists in our youth that shapes us into what we become and how we express ourselves as adults in New Media.

I was always fascinated by pictures as far as I can remember. The ability of one image to tell a story of beauty, sadness or pain is incredible. Taking that image and adding many more images to tell a story in greater depth fascinates me. A film allows us to say what we feel, what makes us happy or sad. It allows us to scream out to to the world our ideas, and opinions about life. It also allows us to create imaginary worlds. I enjoy listening to the ideas of others and working together to bring those ideas to life. Editing a project allows you to change the perspective of a character and tell the story in any manner desired. Putting together the scenes and helping the director or writer’s vision come to life is a skill I have using Adobe Premiere.

As time progressed in college, I was able to appreciate another form of storytelling. I guess my fondness for film and photography led me to another form of media, animation. I enjoyed it as a child, but upon learning hands-on how it was done, it opened my mind to the idea of telling a story in such a manner. Animation allows you to create a different world. The perspective and portrayal of characters is different when done in a real-world production. My skills in Blender and Adobe Illustrator can help another artist take such characters out of that artist’s mind and make them real.

New Media to me means unlimited creativity. I look forward to others sharing their creativity with me and I will do the same. Sometimes in life one has to be a leader. Sometimes one has to be a follower. Whatever the New Media project requires, I will become the individual that is needed for that project’s success.
